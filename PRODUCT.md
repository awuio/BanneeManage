# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Customers browsing the storefront and store owners who use the backend management system to manage categories and content.

## Product Purpose
OmniManage acts as a digital storefront to cleanly display product categories to customers, paired with a robust backend for the owner to effortlessly manage the catalog. Success looks like a frictionless browsing experience for customers and easy administration for the owner.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, modern, and highly professional. Trustworthy and competent without being overbearing.

## Anti-references
Avoid overly playful or whimsical designs. Avoid the "SaaS cliché" of massive rounded corners, gradient text, and unnecessary drop shadows. Do not use cartoonish illustrations. 

## Design Principles
- Clarity over cleverness: get straight to the point.
- Professional restraint: let the content and functionality speak.
- Shadcn aesthetic: clean lines, monochromatic or neutral tones, crisp utility.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. High contrast text (adhering to Shadcn's readable neutrals). Support for prefers-reduced-motion.
